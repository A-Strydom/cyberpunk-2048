HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Combine numbered tiles to reach the 2048 tile! Keep playing to achieve higher scores.

Controls

  • ↑ ↓ ← → Arrow keys to move tiles
  • Swipe on mobile devices

Gameplay

  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one
  • After each move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears randomly
  • Plan your moves strategically to create larger numbers
  • The game ends when the board is full and no moves are possible

Tips

  • Try to keep your highest number in a corner
  • Build up one direction consistently
  • Don't trap your high-value tiles
  • Use the Autodrive button to watch the AI play

Buttons

  • REBOOT - Restart the game
  • Autodrive - Enable AI to play automatically
